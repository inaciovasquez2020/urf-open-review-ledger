Principles
- All reviews, objections, and author responses are public and permanent.
- Frozen manuscript versions are immutable and identified by tag, commit, and hash.
- Technical criticism is never removed.
- Certification, if issued, is version-specific and revocable.

Reviewer Conduct
- Reviews must be technical.
- Objections must be precise and falsifiable.
- Scope disagreement must be labeled as such.

Author Obligations
- Respond to blocking objections publicly.
- Do not modify frozen artifacts; supersede with a new version instead.

Moderation
- Harassment and spam may be removed.
- Technical objections are preserved regardless of outcome.
