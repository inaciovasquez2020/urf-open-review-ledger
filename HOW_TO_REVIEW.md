How to Review a URF Manuscript

This ledger hosts open, technical review of frozen manuscripts.

What to review
- Definitions
- Stated assumptions
- Logical correctness
- Scope and non-claims

What counts as a blocking objection
- An explicit counterexample
- A precisely stated missing lemma or assumption
- A localized proof gap used downstream

What does not count
- Disagreement with scope
- Preference for alternative models
- Empirical performance claims

How to submit a review
- Open a Manuscript Review issue
- Select scope checked
- Give a verdict
- Be precise

Anonymity
- Real name or pseudonym allowed
- Reviews are permanent

Goal
- Clarify what is true
- Identify exact boundaries
- Preserve a public technical record
