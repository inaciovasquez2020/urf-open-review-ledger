\section*{Axiom Schema (SG--Closure)}

For every admissible URF system $(H,\Delta_H,\mathrm{Per})$:

\textbf{(A) Compact Resolvent}
\[
(\Delta_H + I)^{-1} \in \mathcal{K}(H).
\]

\textbf{(B) Geometric Unitary Realization}
\[
\exists\ (M,g)\ \text{compact},\ \exists\ U:H\to L^2(M)\ \text{unitary such that}
\quad \Delta_H = U^{-1}(-\Delta_g)U.
\]

\textbf{(C) Projection Alignment}
\[
\mathrm{Per} = U^{-1}\Pi_{\mathrm{const}}U.
\]

\[
\inf \mathrm{Spec}(\Delta_H \mid \ker(\mathrm{Per})^\perp) > 0.
\]

\section*{Impossibility Corollary}

Under (A)--(C), failure of URF\_law3 is logically impossible in ZFC.
