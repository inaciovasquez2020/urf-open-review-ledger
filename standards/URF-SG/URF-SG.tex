\section*{URF-SG — Spectral Gap Rigidity Standard (v1.0)}

\textbf{Status:} Conditional institutional standard.

\textbf{Invariant.}
There exists $\alpha>0$ such that for all
$f\perp\ker(\mathrm{Per})$:
\[
\langle f,\mathcal H_\Phi f\rangle \ge \alpha \|f\|^2.
\]

\textbf{Finite Certificate.}
Let $\ell_1,\dots,\ell_m$ be linear observables and define:
\[
G_{ij}=\langle \nabla \ell_i,\nabla \ell_j\rangle.
\]

A valid URF-SG certificate satisfies:
\[
\lambda_{\min}(G) \ge \alpha > 0.
\]

\textbf{Verification.}
Certificates are machine-verifiable using \texttt{verify.py}.
