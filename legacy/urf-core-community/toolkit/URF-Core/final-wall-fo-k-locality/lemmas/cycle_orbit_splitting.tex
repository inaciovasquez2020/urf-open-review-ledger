\section*{Cycle--Orbit Splitting Lemma}

Let $\Delta(G)\le \Delta$ and define:

\[
\mathsf{CycComp}_R(G)
=\max_v \dim_{\mathbb F_2} Z_1(B_{2R}(v)).
\]

Then there exists $t^*(k,\Delta,R)$ such that:

\[
\mathsf{CycComp}_R(G)\ge t^*
\;\Rightarrow\;
\exists v,w:\;
\mathrm{tp}^{\mathrm{FO}^k}_R(v)\neq \mathrm{tp}^{\mathrm{FO}^k}_R(w).
\]

Proof uses the Cycle Fan Predicate and EF Cycle-Linkage Trap.
