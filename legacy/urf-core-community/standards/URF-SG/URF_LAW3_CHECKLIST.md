# URF_law3 Admissibility Checklist

To claim URF applicability, you must prove:

## (A) Compact Resolvent
Show:
(Δ + I)^{-1} is compact.

Equivalent:
- Discrete spectrum
- Eigenvalues → ∞

## (B) Unitary Equivalence
Exhibit:
U : H → L²(M)
such that:
Δ = U^{-1} Δ_g U

## (C) Projection Alignment
Show:
Per = U^{-1} Π_const U

## If ANY fail:
URF_law3 does not apply.

## If ALL hold:
Spectral gap is guaranteed.
No counterexample exists.
