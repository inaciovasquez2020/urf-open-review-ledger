# Final Wall / Conditional Index Glossary

**Admissibility Boundary**  
A boundary separating admissible from inadmissible processes under URF-ADMISSIBLE.

**Closure Boundary**  
A point in a reduction where all remaining progress requires explicitly stated extra assumptions.

**Conditional Dependency**  
A named missing lemma/inequality whose truth is required for a claimed implication.

**Conditional Index**  
A curated index of conditional dependencies and where they are used.

**Conditional Closure**  
A closure statement explicitly labeled as conditional on listed dependencies.

**Final Wall**  
A terminal statement asserting that, under specified constraints, further refinement/distinction cannot exceed a stated limit.

**Local–Global Barrier**  
A statement that bounded-local information cannot determine a global property within the admissible class.

**Refinement Limit**  
A quantitative bound on what a refinement process can accomplish (e.g., depth, entropy drop, distinguishability).

**Sufficiency Certificate**  
A certificate asserting a condition is sufficient for a downstream claim under stated hypotheses.

**Terminal Wall**  
A boundary beyond which the admissible process cannot proceed without violating constraints or importing new axioms.

**Witness Schema**  
A schema specifying the structure of a NO witness or obstruction certificate.
