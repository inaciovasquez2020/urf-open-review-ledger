# NCR / ISR Glossary

**Computational Regime**  
A class of behaviors defined by invariant properties of refinement/verification under constraints.

**ISR (Normalization-Resistant Regime)**  
A regime in which normalization does not collapse the relevant distinguishing structure.

**Normalization Resistance**  
Persistence of a separating invariant under allowed normal-form transforms.

**Regime Boundary**  
A condition separating two regimes (e.g., where an invariant begins/ceases to exist).

**Signed Divergence Witness**  
A signed artifact witnessing minimal divergence/separation under the verifier rules.

**Trace State**  
A structured record of intermediate states used for auditing refinement behavior.

**Trace State Structural Invariant**  
A property of trace states preserved across steps (counts, nodes, structural fields).
